  
Leticia Cáceres is an Argentine/Australian screen and stage director.  Leticia was Associate Director for Melbourne Theatre Company and Queensland Theatre Company. She has directed opera, musicals, and new Australian dramas for which she has won Helpmanns (*The Drovers Wife, Barbara and the Camp Dogs*), Greenroom (*Birdland*) and Sydney Theatre Awards (*The Drovers Wife*).   For television, Leticia has directed two blocks of Stan’s Original Series *BUMP* produced by Roadshow Rough Diamond; *SPOOKY FILES* produced by Tony Ayres Productions for ABC and BBC; *EROTIC STORIES* produced by Lingo Pictures in association with ITV Studio for SBS and on SBS OnDemand; *Rock Island Mysteries* Season 3, produced by Fremantle Media and Network 10; *RFDS* Season 3 produced by Endemol Shine for Seven Network. Her screen work has been nominated for an Australian Directors Guild award, Australian Academy of Cinema and Television Awards and LOGIE Awards. Leticia is currently developing a feature film script with writer Claire Phillips and producer Miriam Stein of Tama Films, with assistance from Screen NSW. In 2025 Leticia wrote and directed *Weight Limit*, a 13-minute short exploring her personal experience of migration from 1980s Argentina, reimagined in a contemporary Latin/ Australian context.  In 2026 Leticia will return to the stage to direct *Losing Face* by Marieke Hardy for Melbourne Theatre Company.
