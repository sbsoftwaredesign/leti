## Profile

Award-winning Australian director for screen and stage with over 20 years of experience. Television credits spanning drama, comedy, and children's programming for Stan, Network Seven, SBS, ABC, Nickelodeon, and BBC. Short films screened and awarded at international festivals including MIFF, London, Wellington, and Vancouver. Winner of multiple Helpmann Awards, Green Room Awards, and Sydney Theatre Awards. Nominated for AACTA, LOGIE, and ADG Awards. Over 30 professional stage productions for Australia's leading theatre companies.

## Education

**Foundations in Film & Television** — VCA, University of Melbourne (2017)
**Master of Fine Arts, Drama Direction** — VCA, University of Melbourne (2014)
**Bachelor of Arts, Drama (Honours)** — Queensland University of Technology (1999)

## Professional Positions

**Associate Director** — Melbourne Theatre Company (2012–2015)
Directed mainstage and education productions. Implemented MTC's Women Directors Program. Curated education season and developed pathways for emerging artists.

**Artistic Director** — Tantrum Theatre, Newcastle (2006–2008)
Artistic programming, directing, strategic planning. National touring and community engagement.

**Associate & Intern Director** — Queensland Theatre Company (2003–2005)
Mainstage and education directing. New writing development and regional programs.

**Co-Founder & Co-Director** — Real TV, Melbourne (2000–2020)
Independent production company. Nationally and internationally touring productions.

## Grants & Fellowships

- Screen Australia — Gender Matters / Brilliant Careers (2017)
