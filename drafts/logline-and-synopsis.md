**Logline**

Faced with an unplanned pregnancy, two teenagers set out on the road together, only to discover what they are truly seeking is each other. 

**Synopsis**

Precocious 16-year-old JEM is determined to make it out of her small country town. As the only daughter to CHRISTINE, a single mum and a cleaner, who fell pregnant with Jem when she was still a teen, Jem is determined to avoid her mum’s mistake – which is why she’s horrified to discover she’s pregnant. 

Jem hashes a plan. She rocks up on her best friend HARRY’s doorstep and convinces him to steal his mum’s beat up 2005 Toyota Tarago and drive her to Sydney to have an abortion. 

Jem and Harry lie to their parents and hit the road. But they quickly discover this road trip isn’t gonna be easy. Not only is the Tarago barely roadworthy– but they’ve been forced to bring along Christine’s beloved pet rabbit, who’s sick and requires critical care nursing. 

Jem needs cash for the abortion, so she reluctantly calls the “oops” baby’s father \- BOMBER, the boarding school jock she had a fling with over summer. But as sparks reignite between them, Harry feels jealous.

When Jem and Harry are forced to torch the Tarago to cover their tracks, their true feelings come to the surface, and they kiss. But instead of bringing them together, it forces them apart. Jem, scared, confused, leaves Harry, adamant she’s blown her life up. 

Jem’s mum discovers Jem’s been lying to her. She travels to Sydney to bring Jem home forcing Jem to make a choice –tell her mum the truth or have the abortion alone.
