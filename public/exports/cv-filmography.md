# Leticia Cáceres

## Director

Cameron's Management · Joel Pettersson, joel.pettersson@cameronsmanagement.com.au · [www.leticiacaceres.com](https://www.leticiacaceres.com)

## Profile

Award-winning Australian director for screen and stage with over 20 years of experience. Television credits spanning drama, comedy, and children's programming for Stan, Network Seven, SBS, ABC, Nickelodeon, and BBC. Short films screened and awarded at international festivals including MIFF, London, Wellington, and Vancouver. Winner of multiple Helpmann, Green Room, and Sydney Theatre Awards. Nominated for AACTA, LOGIE, and ADG Awards. Over 30 professional stage productions for Australia's leading theatre companies.
